ELENA FENG

What interests me most in life is its sense of reality. When I listen carefully to the stories around me, I find the most amazing inspiration. Everyone is wonderful and unique, I like to journey through their stories. As a fashion designer, reducing gender is what I have been pursuing through my collections. This is not only a simple way for men to wear skirts or give women an oversized wide shoulder jacket but also enables them to have more experiences to touch and feel more fabrics and textures and to express themselves through silhouettes, to show their emotions through layers. This is who I am as a Designer, a designer who gives her audience a choice. What richens my work is the experience that I have been given by life every day.
